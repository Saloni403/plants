About Us

We don’t just sell plants — we help you build a profitable, world-class orchard. Partner with us today for guaranteed success!

The Green Orchard is a government-registered plant nursery located in Managam Village, Tehsil Wagoora, District Baramulla, Jammu & Kashmir, India.

We are officially registered with the Department of Horticulture under Registration No. 2025-K-904528.

Our Mission

provide farmers and orchardists with healthy, genetically superior, and certified virus-free planting material that ensures high productivity and long-term profitability.

We are committed to delivering premium fruit plants, advanced trellis systems, and expert technical guidance so that every grower can establish a modern, sustainable, and world-class orchard with confidence.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and innovative orchard development company, known for transforming traditional farming into highly productive, high-density orchard systems.

We aim to empower growers nationwide by offering international-quality rootstocks, the latest fruit varieties, and lifetime agronomic support, ensuring a future where every orchard thrives with excellence, efficiency, and global standards.

Healthy, disease-free, and genetically superior planting material.
