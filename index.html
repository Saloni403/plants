About Us

The Green Orchard is a government-registered plant nursery located in Managam Village, Tehsil Wagoora, District Baramulla, Jammu & Kashmir, India. We are officially registered with the Department of Horticulture under Registration No. 2025-K-904528

We specialize in high-quality fruit plants and rootstocks grafted on premium clonal and seedling rootstock varieties, including M9T337, MM111, Geneva, Quince, Myrobalan, and others.

Our extensive collection features true-to-type, high-yielding fruit plants such as: Apple, Cherry, Pear, Peach, Almond, Walnut, Persimmon, Kiwi and many more.

In addition to grafted fruit plants, we offer certified, true-to-type clonal rootstocks including M9T337, MM111, Geneva series, and the latest international varieties trusted by progressive orchardists.

At The Green Orchard, we are committed to providing healthy, disease-free, and genetically superior planting material to help farmers and growers establish productive and profitable orchards.

Why Choose Green Orchard?

  • Grown Locally:
    All our plant material comes directly from our own Nursery, established and nurtured in the exact same soil and climate as your orchard.
  • Zero Transplant Shock, Maximum Survival
    Growers can personally select and uproot plants from our nursery and transplant them the same day. Because the plants are already hardened to local conditions, they establish quickly with minimal mortality and superior initial growth.
  • Direct-from-Nursery Pricing – No Hidden Costs

  • 100% True-to-Type & Virus-Free Guarantee
    Every plant is produced under strict supervision using genuine imported rootstocks and scions, ensuring authenticity, health, and outstanding performance in your fields.
